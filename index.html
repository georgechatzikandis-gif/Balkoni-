Our Story

Perched above the old town, poured with care

Balcony 961 sits above Ippokratous Square, where cobblestones, centuries-old stone and golden light meet. By day it's your espresso stop overlooking the fountain; by night the balcony fills with mint, citrus and conversation as the sun drops behind the rooftops.
